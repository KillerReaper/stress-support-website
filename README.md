# Stress Management Strategies for College Students

## Project Overview
This project is a health promotion website created for a course assignment in **Stress Management (HEED-213-600)** at **Howard Community College**.

The purpose of this website is to educate college students on effective stress management techniques in a clear, simple, and practical way.

**Instructor:** Melanie Nystrom, MA

---

## Target Population
College students who experience stress from academic pressure, deadlines, and balancing responsibilities.

---

## Stress Management Techniques Covered
This website focuses on three key stress management techniques that are highly relevant for college students:

- **Deep Breathing**  
  A quick and simple technique that helps calm the body and reduce anxiety.

- **Social Support**  
  Seeking help, encouragement, and connection with others to manage emotional stress.

- **Time Management**  
  Organizing tasks and responsibilities to reduce overwhelm and improve productivity.

---

## Website Structure
The website is designed as a multi-page experience with clear navigation:

- **Home Page**
  - Introduction to stress and its impact on college students  
  - Overview of all three techniques  

- **Deep Breathing Page**
  - Explanation of the technique  
  - Step-by-step instructions  
  - Why it works  
  - When to use it  

- **Social Support Page**
  - Definition and types of support  
  - Why it works  
  - Real-life examples for students  

- **Time Management Page**
  - Key strategies  
  - Why it works  
  - Practical applications for students
