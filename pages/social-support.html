Social Support for Stress Management

Social support means connecting with people who can offer encouragement, understanding, and practical help during stressful times. Feeling supported can make challenges feel more manageable.

Why It Helps

Talking with others can reduce feelings of isolation and remind students that they do not have to manage stress alone. Supportive conversations can also help students see problems from a new perspective.

Examples of Social Support

  • Talking with a friend or family member
  • Studying with classmates
  • Reaching out to a professor, advisor, or counselor
