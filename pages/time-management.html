Time Management for Stress Management

Time management helps students organize responsibilities, reduce last-minute pressure, and create a healthier balance between school, work, and personal life.

Why It Matters

When tasks are planned ahead of time, students are less likely to feel overwhelmed by deadlines. Good time management can improve focus, lower stress, and make daily routines more manageable.

Helpful Strategies

  • Use a planner or digital calendar
  • Break large assignments into smaller tasks
  • Set priorities and create a realistic schedule
