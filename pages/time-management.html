Managing Stress with Time Management

Time management means organizing tasks and using time wisely so responsibilities feel more manageable. For college students, it can reduce stress, improve balance, and help create a stronger sense of control.

What This Technique Is

Time management is the process of planning how to spend your time and deciding which tasks need attention first. It helps students stay organized, avoid unnecessary pressure, and keep up with academic and personal responsibilities in a more steady way.

When You Would Use It

  • When feeling overwhelmed with assignments
  • When deadlines are approaching
  • When procrastinating
  • When balancing multiple responsibilities

How and Why It Works

  • Reduces last-minute stress
  • Increases sense of control
  • Improves focus and productivity
  • Prevents workload from building up

How to Do It

  • Make a daily or weekly schedule
  • Prioritize important tasks first
  • Break large assignments into smaller steps
  • Set realistic goals and deadlines

Try It Now

Write down your top 3 tasks for today and complete them in order.
